About Us

Greening Los Osos (GLO) is a community-based, community-driven organization created in February 2019 to preserve, maintain and enhance our local tree population San Luis Obispo County.

GLO maintains nonprofit staus through our fiscal sponsor, Ecologistics, a 501(c)3 organization.
