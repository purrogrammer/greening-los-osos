Greening Los Osos

one tree at a time...

The Central Coast of California

has faced a crisis of deforestation in recent years due to drought, fires, development, and more. Together, we can and must replant and rebuild. That's why Greening Los Osos was founded.

Protecting Nature

We work with public agencies, San Luis Obispo County, and other groups to advocate for tree preservation and planting new trees.

Planting Trees

Join one of our hands-on, volunteer-driven community tree-planting events!


Education & Outreach

We work with community members and local schools to provide resources on which native trees will thrive in our area, and when, how and where to plant them.

About Us

Greening Los Osos (GLO) is a community-based, community-driven organization created in February 2019 to preserve, maintain and enhance our local tree population on both public and private lands.

GLO maintains nonprofit staus through our fiscal sponsor, Ecologistics, a 501(c)3 organization.
